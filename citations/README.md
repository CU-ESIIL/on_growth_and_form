# Citations

Keep tracked notes and export files here. Put article PDFs in `pdfs/`, which is ignored by Git.

- `notes/`: provenance notes, reading notes, and verification status for references.
- `exports/`: BibTeX and other sharable citation exports.

When importing citations from working notes or proposal brainstorming, keep them marked as unverified until they are checked against the original source, publisher page, DOI record, or PDF.
