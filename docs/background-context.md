# Background and Context

This section collects the scientific framing that helps explain why the proposal matters, what intellectual traditions it draws on, and how its modeling ideas fit into broader theory. It is intentionally separate from `funder/`, which is where the call, the briefing, and requirement tracking live.

!!! note "Why this exists"
    `funder/` answers "what is the call asking for?" Background/context answers "what ideas, scientific patterns, and conceptual tools help us shape a strong answer?"

## How to use this section

- Use these notes to draft human-readable background sections.
- Draw from them when explaining theory, scaling, geometry, modeling architecture, or intellectual lineage.
- Keep them interpretive and synthetic rather than compliance-oriented.
