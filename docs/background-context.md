# Background and Context

This page tracks contextual and strategic framing notes that support proposal writing but are intentionally kept separate from sponsor-controlled material.

!!! note "Why this exists"
    `funder/` is for official or sponsor-provided documents and direct requirement summaries. `background_context/` is for our own synthesis, positioning, and contextual interpretation.

- Keep interpretive language separate from the solicitation itself
