# Theory

This section lays out the conceptual structure of the project. It begins from a broad question about growth and form, then narrows toward a wildfire-specific scaling hypothesis, the idea of regime-specific behavior during fire expansion, and the open scientific questions that still need to be tested.

## Narrative arc

The theory section is organized around a simple progression:

1. **Form**: why geometry and physical constraint matter
2. **Hypothesis**: why wildfire growth may exhibit emergent scaling
3. **Regimes**: why that scaling may apply only during part of a fire lifetime
4. **Questions**: what still needs to be measured and explained
