# Models

This section connects the wildfire scaling idea to actual modeling frameworks.

## Why this section matters

The proposal is not trying to replace fire modeling with abstract geometry. It is asking how existing and simplified models can be interpreted through the lens of scaling, interface growth, and emergent perimeter form.
